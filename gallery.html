3D Art using Meshmixer

Halloween Cat (1024 x 768)

There resides a peculiar statue of a cat at the abandoned house a bit away from the others. This cat is no ordinary cat as it has two extra tails and horns sticking out of its little witches hat. Though it has a strange look, the mystery of the cat is even stranger. Every year, on Halloween day, the cat disappears without a trace behind, then the day after, the cat is back as if it were never gone in the first place. The oddest occurrences happen Halloween night. The webs that adorn the decorated houses end up on the street, the lamps flicker at an unusual pace, and the children are saddened that their candy mysteriously disappears every year. Where does it end up you may ask? Why, in the pumpkin that forever stands next to that mysterious cat.

Digital Video

Burnout (1920 x 1080)

Burnout is my favorite video that I created. It is about how draining it could be to a student and how they struggle to stay focused. Students would rather do anything else than pay attention. This is because they are experiencing burnout. Even when the person in the video was trying to study, they couldn't comprehend anything they were reading. Only when this student goes outside, away from the draining environment of the library, does the tone of the video change. They feel at peace and as if they were free. I myself was experiencing burnout during the filming of this video, which I hinted to at the end. This video was filmed with my phone and edited together uding Adobe Premiere Pro. I recorded the sounds with Voice Memos then edited it into a cohesive soundtrack. Click on the title to see the video!


A Day at the Library (1280 x 720)

First, I filmed multiple clips using my phone. This was before I purchased a professional camera but I feel like it adds to the nature of this video. I used Adobe Premiere Pro to edit the clips together as well as add background music. The people in the video are actually my roommates. Click on the title to see the video!


Sound 1 (1920 x 1080)

I recorded unique sounds using Voice Memos. I used Adobe Audition to edit the sounds in a a random order. I then recorded clips of one of my roommates trying to match the audio track I created. I created a video based on the sound. The last step was to edit the clips together in Premiere Pro. Finally, I added the audio track to the video. Click on the title to see the video!


Unerving (1920 x 1080)

Similarly to Sound 1, I recorded the sounds and created the audio track before I recorded the video. I used the same sounds however, I edited them together differently. This audiotrack was almost horror-like. This inspired me to film an unerving and gritty video. Click on the title to see the video!
